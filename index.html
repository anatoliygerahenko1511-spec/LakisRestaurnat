Our Story

Some places you eat.
LAKI's is a place
you come home to.

Before LAKI's opened its doors in February 2020, owner Laki Dalios was already a familiar face around Smithville, having run a much-loved restaurant here years earlier. After a quiet stretch away, Laki couldn't stay gone for long.

He came back, took over the building that once housed Rolly's Smokehouse, and turned it into something the town had been missing: a true family restaurant.

These days you'll often catch Laki himself stopping by your table, asking how the food is, trading a story, making sure you leave a little fuller and a lot happier than you came in. That's the whole idea here. Honest food, made from scratch, served by people who are genuinely glad you walked in.
